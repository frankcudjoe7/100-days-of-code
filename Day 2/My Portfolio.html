About My Country

Ghana (/ˈɡɑːnə/ (About this soundlisten)), officially the Republic of Ghana, is a country along the Gulf of Guinea and the Atlantic Ocean, in the subregion of West Africa. Spanning a land mass
of 238,535 km2 (92,099 sq mi), Ghana is bordered by the Ivory Coast in the west, Burkina Faso in the north, Togo in the east, the Gulf of Guinea and the Atlantic Ocean in the south.
Ghana means "Warrior King" in the Soninke language.

Currently, the President of Ghana is His Excellency Nana Akufo-Addo
